## Exercise

following $2 \times 2$ system of linear equation provided that $ad – bc$ is not 0:

$$
ax + by = e\\
cx+dy=f \\
$$

$$
x = \frac{ed - bf}{ad-bc}, \ y = \frac{af - ec}{ad - bc}
$$

y: (Hint: replace the symbols in the formula with numbers to compute x and y. This
exercise can be done in Chapter 1 without using materials in later chapters.)

$$
3.4x + 50.2y = 44.5 \\
2.1x+.55y = 5.9
